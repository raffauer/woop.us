Yet another title

raffael has won his 3rd consecutive effic title just some months ago, and on last Sunday we have won our 3rd consecutive eCTF title.

Even without one of our most important players (Acuerta) we managed to clinch a 3rd consecutive title in yet another, 3rd in a row w00p vs sp4nk final. Our teamplay (lagout, raffael, Honzik1) was impressive, and we haven't lost a single map in the tourney. Sadly, once again sp4nk didn't manage to field their best team, and this time they even had worse lineup than last time - they had only starch from their most potent lineup.
Honzik1 wanted me to point out that his GPU was burning freshly before the semifinal match against !s (rumors say that he was taking too many rockets from raffael in a duel match) so he had to play with the integrated graphics card running on 100 fps.
